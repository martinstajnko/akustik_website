About

Artistik is an acoustic group consisting of five young academic musicians on Flute, Violin/Vocal, Cello, Piano and Cajon/Clarinet/Guitar. We play popular and original music, prepared in our own unique way. Our music adds charm and quality to events of all kinds. We also work with excellent singers.
